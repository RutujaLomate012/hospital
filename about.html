More about NIMS

NIMS Hospital is the Accident Hospital in Nashik and Emergency Centre that is known for responding quickly in times of crashes, heart attacks, paralytic strokes, and other life-threatening situations, providing the afflicted with the best possibilities of survival and rehabilitation. Because emergencies and catastrophes occur without notice, treatment cannot be delayed.

Our cutting-edge, fully equipped Emergency Medical Services facility is fully prepared with full-time consultants to providespecialised and advanced medical care for all types of accidents and emergencies around the clock, for the prevention,diagnosis, and management of acute and urgent aspects of illness and injury affecting patients of all ages and with a fullspectrum of undifferentiated physical and behavioural symptoms. The NIMS emergency department is staffed by a team ofphysicians who have advanced procedural skills such as surgical operations, trauma resuscitation, and advanced cardiac lifesupport. If you are in search of Accident Hospital in Nashik, Visit NIMS Hospital.

NIMS Hospital has tied up with several leading insurance providers to offer cashless hospitalization to patients. This means that patients who are covered under an insurance policy can avail of medical treatment without paying any cash upfront. The hospital will directly bill the insurance provider for the medical expenses incurred during the hospitalization.

Financial Security:The insurance and cashless facility provide patients with financial security during medical emergencies. They can receive the necessary medical treatment without worrying about the financial burden.
