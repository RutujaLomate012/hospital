Dr. Gaurav Roy

He has also acquired fellowship in advanced endoscopy, laser and skull vessel ENT surgery. Dr. Roy has been providing ENT related services for the past 10 years. He has acquired expertise in endoscopic ENT surgery, laser and coblation surgery, which are considered to be state-of-the-art in ENT. Also Dr. Roy hails from Sunny, Mumbai in Muscle Fashion Trauma Surgery.
