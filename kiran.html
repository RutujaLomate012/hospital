Dr. Kiran Chintaman Patil

Dr. Kiran Chintaman Patil is most recommended Physician at Gangapur road in entire Nashik city also. His native place is Nandgaon, District-Nashik. His father was a Central Government Employee.

Dr.Kiran Patil took his primary education from R.V.Shah school and Obtained secondary and higher secondary education from KBH Vidyalaya Malegaon.

He achieved a good rank in MAHARASHTRA COMMON ENTRANCE (CET) AND got admission in MBBS Course at the prestigious Government Medical College, Miraj.

He scored First-Class in his final MBBS and completed his Internship at Padmabhushan Vasant Dada Hospital, Sangli. He got trained for basic procedures under legends in the field at Sangli Hospital.

Pursuing his interest in the field of Medicine he took the admission for Post-Graduation in Medicine at Ashwini Sahakari Rugnalaya and Research Center, Solapur. He passed and got a Diplomate of National Board (DNB, New Delhi) in the Branch of Internal Medicine which is supposed to be a very difficult exam to pass.

Over the last 10 years. He is practicing in Nashik City. For the initial 4 years, he was consultant Physician at Saptashrungi, and Riddhi hospitals.

For last 5 years he is working as a Director and full-time Physician at NIMS HOSPITAL, Nashik.

He has successfully treated thousands of patients for various diseases like diabetes, hypertension, dengue, swine flu, thyroid gland diseases, lung diseases, and infectious diseases.

He is expert in handling all type of acute emergencies like stroke, heart attack, ARDS etc.

He is life member of IMA AND ISCCM

He renders his specialized services as a consultant physician in all facilities like ICU, OPD, and Dialysis Unit IPD. He intends to make NIMS Hospital ICU and Intensive Care the most advanced in the Nashik district in the coming time.
