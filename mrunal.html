Dr. Mrunal Kapadnis: Top Obstetrician, Gynecologist & Infertility Specialist in Nashik

Dr. Mrunal Kapadnis is a highly respected Obstetrician, Gynecologist, and Infertility Specialist in Nashik, known for her compassionate nature and patient-friendly approach. With over a decade of experience in the field, she is dedicated to providing world-class gynecology and obstetrics care.

She completed her MBBS degree from Aurangabad Government College and Ghati Hospital and her MS in Gynecology and Obstetrics from JJ Hospital and Grant Medical College in Mumbai, prestigious medical institutions in India.

With three years of experience working alongside renowned gynecologists and infertility specialists at institutions like JJ Hospital, Kama Hospital, and St. George Hospital, Dr. Kapadnis gained invaluable expertise. She further honed her skills and knowledge as an assistant professor at Lokmanya Tilak Medical College, Sion, Mumbai for two years.

Dr. Kapadnis has successfully managed numerous vaginal and cesarean deliveries, including high-risk cases such as twins, triplets, low-lying placenta, and pregnancies with diabetes, hypertension, cardiac problems, epilepsy, and more. She completed a fellowship in high-risk pregnancy management at Sion Hospital, Mumbai, further specializing in managing complex pregnancies.

Additionally, Dr. Kapadnis is a fellow of minimal access surgery (FMAS) from Bangalore, where she mastered laparoscopic techniques for surgeries such as uterine and ovarian tumor removal, dermoid cysts, ectopic pregnancies, and fertility-enhancing procedures. She has received specialized training in the treatment of uterine and ovarian cancer at Kama Hospital, Mumbai.

For the past nine years, Dr. Kapadnis has been serving the community of Nashik at the Siddhidata Urology and Maternity Center on Sharanpur Road. She currently holds the position of Head and Director of the Department of Gynecology and Obstetrics at Nims Hospital and Research Center on Gangapur Road.

Dr. Kapadnis believes in alleviating her patients’ pain and sorrows by offering personalized care and support. She takes the time to listen to her patients, understand their concerns, and provide them with the best possible treatment options. Her compassionate nature ensures that her patients feel comfortable, cared for, and supported throughout their healthcare journey.

At her practice, Dr. Kapadnis has assembled a highly skilled team of surgeons, intensivists, and pediatricians who share her commitment to delivering exceptional care. The practice is fully equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including an in-house pharmacy, laboratory, and sonography services. This comprehensive approach ensures that patients receive comprehensive and convenient care under one roof.

Furthermore, Dr. Kapadnis understands the urgency and importance of timely medical attention. That’s why her practice offers 24/7 ambulance availability, ensuring that patients can access immediate medical assistance whenever needed.

With her compassionate and patient-friendly approach, Dr. Mrunal Kapadnis has earned the trust and admiration of her patients. She goes above and beyond to provide the highest standard of care, making a positive impact on the lives of her patients and their families.
