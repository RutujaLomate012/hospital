Dr. Parag Dhamane

Dr. Parag Dhamane, a native of Lohoner, Deola, taluka, graduated from Government College, Thane and Rajiv Gandhi Medical College. He then completed his Post Graduate DNB education from MOSC Medical College, Hospital, Cochin.

And from MBBS he was very interested in surgery. He worked in the surgical department of Bombay Hospital and later joined MOSC Medical College in Cochin. Later he worked as a lecturer at Pravara Medical College and learned laparoscopy and advanced surgery during a 3-year stint at Wockhardt Hospital in Nashik. Also he has fellowship Minimal Access Surgery(FMAS)

He has been serving as General Surgeon in many private and corporate hospitals in Nashik since 5 years. He also has an OPD at Aryan Clinic, Trimurti chowk and has published several international papers on surgery. He is a member of the Indian Surgical Society and a member of the Indian Colorectal Surgeons.

He has served numerous patients and performed major surgeries in Nashik over the last 7 years and is currently working as the Director of Nims Hospital.

Dr. Parag Dhamane operates all kinds of simple and complex hernias.

Laparoscopically all major surgery ecg lap appendectomy ,lap cholecystectomy.

He has operated on a number of patients of piles, fissure, fistula in ano and pilonidal sinus.
