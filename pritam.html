Dr. Pritam Pawar

Dr. Pritam Ratnakant Pawar is a native of Saundane in Malegaon. He did his primary education at Deola and secondary education at Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Khedgaon Taluka Dindori and completed his 11th and 12th from KTHM College. Later he completed his MBBS degree from Dr. DY Patil Medical College, Kolhapur.

After that he trained and graduated in Orthopedics from the famous Chhatrapati Pramila Raje Hospital, Kolhapur.

In 2006, he started a private service in Malegaon. A year later, he started his own Pawar Accident Hospital in Malegaon. Since 2014 Dr. Pawar resides in Nashik and is working as Director at Nims Hospital, Gangapur Road. Dr. Pritam Pawar specializes in the comprehensive treatment of various types of fractures, such as hairline fractures, spiral fractures, and numerous others. He is a full time member of Maharashtra Medical Council and Indian Medical Council.

During a period of 17 years, he successfully treated and operated thousands of patients mainly in Kasamade. The doctor has performed particularly well in the field of Arthritis, rheumatism, joint replacement, Diseases of the nerves, Bone diseases in children.
