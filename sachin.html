Dr. Sachin Deore

Dr. Sachin Devre is a native of Karanjad in Satana taluka and completed his primary education in Malegaon. Father of Laparoscopic Surgery Professor Sir Dr. at Breach Candy Hospital in Mumbai after completing his MBBS degree from MIT College, Talegaon, Pune. Under the guidance of Udwadia, he received training in general and laparoscopy surgery.
